# LoveNest — Emotional Onboarding System

## Product Architecture & Experience Direction

---

# OBJECTIVE

Create a world-class emotional onboarding experience for LoveNest.

This onboarding must NOT feel like:

* a tutorial
* a feature walkthrough
* a productivity app intro
* a dating app introduction

LoveNest is not sold through features.

LoveNest is sold through:

* emotional atmosphere
* emotional continuity
* emotional presence
* quiet intimacy
* shared rituals
* emotional memory

The onboarding experience must emotionally communicate:

> “This is a private emotional space for two people.”

The goal is emotional connection BEFORE product explanation.

---

# CORE EXPERIENCE PRINCIPLE

The onboarding should feel like:

* a calm emotional invitation
* a cinematic emotional pause
* a quiet beginning

NOT:

* an app tutorial
* a marketing slideshow
* an explanation carousel

---

# EXPERIENCE FEELING

The user should feel:

* calm
* emotionally curious
* emotionally safe
* emotionally invited
* emotionally understood

The experience should feel:

* premium
* minimalist
* modern
* intimate
* breathable
* emotionally intelligent

---

# REFERENCES

The onboarding atmosphere should feel inspired by:

* Apple onboarding
* Calm
* Headspace
* Airbnb
* Notion
* Linear

NOT:

* Tinder
* Duolingo
* gaming apps
* productivity onboarding
* social media tutorials

---

# IMPORTANT DESIGN RULES

## DO:

* use large breathing spaces
* use minimal copy
* use emotional typography
* use soft transitions
* use subtle ambient motion
* preserve emotional silence
* keep focus on emotional feeling

---

## DO NOT:

* use feature-heavy slides
* use illustrations of couples
* use cliché hearts
* use emojis
* use loud gradients
* use visual overload
* use excessive icons
* use marketing language

---

The onboarding must contain:

Reason:

* preserves emotional impact
* reduces fatigue
* increases completion rate
* keeps emotional clarity

# MOTION SYSTEM

## IMPORTANT

Motion must feel:

* cinematic
* calm
* soft
* emotionally intelligent

NOT:

* flashy
* gaming-like
* reactive
* exaggerated

---

# SCREEN TRANSITIONS

Use:

* fade transitions
* subtle slide movement
* opacity interpolation
* gentle timing

Suggested:

* duration: 400–600ms
* easing: ease-out soft

---

# ELEMENT MOTION

Allowed:

* floating motion
* breathing glow
* slow particle movement
* subtle orbit movement

Avoid:

* bounce
* fast movement
* spring-heavy motion
* large parallax
* strong scale animations

---

# TYPOGRAPHY

Typography should be:

* emotional
* spacious
* elegant
* readable
* calm

---

## Main Text

* large
* centered
* emotional emphasis
* high breathing room

---

## Secondary Text

* soft graphite
* smaller weight
* maximum 2 lines
* low visual pressure

---

# COLOR SYSTEM

ONLY use:

* warm white
* soft graphite
* muted rose
* soft coral
* ultra subtle ambient gradients

Avoid:

* yellow
* neon
* saturated colors
* rainbow gradients

---

# BUTTON SYSTEM

Buttons should feel:

* soft
* premium
* tactile
* calm

NOT:

* oversized
* hyper colorful
* gaming-like

---

# PAGINATION

Use:

* ultra minimal indicators

Examples:

* soft dots
* tiny progress line

Low visual priority.

---

# AUDIO & HAPTICS

Optional:

* tiny soft haptic on transitions

DO NOT:

* use sounds
* use aggressive vibration

---

# IMPLEMENTATION STRATEGY

## IMPORTANT

Implement onboarding in phases.

DO NOT build everything at once.

---

# PHASE 1

Build:

* onboarding structure
* screens
* navigation
* typography
* spacing

NO advanced motion yet.

---

# PHASE 2

Add:

* ambient motion
* subtle transitions
* floating elements
* breathing animations

---

# PHASE 3

Add:

* invite flow
* account connection
* emotional personalization

---

# PERFORMANCE RULES

Keep onboarding:

* lightweight
* smooth
* mobile-first
* low memory usage

Avoid:

* heavy animation libraries
* excessive re-renders
* video backgrounds

---

# FINAL EXPERIENCE GOAL

The onboarding should end with the user feeling:

> “This feels emotionally different.”

NOT:

> “I learned how the app works.”

That emotional distinction is the foundation of the LoveNest identity.
